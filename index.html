About Us

Best Engineering College in Kerala:KMCT Institute of Emerging Technology & Management

KMCT Institute of Emerging Technology and Management is a prestigious engineering college in Kozhikode, with a key determination to provide the technology of tomorrow. The institution offers four B.Tech programmes, approved by AICTE and affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, Kerala, ensuring students receive quality engineering education. In addition to B.Tech Programs, the college also offers BBA, BCA, and Diploma in Engineering programs.Situated at Mukkam, Kozhikode, Kerala, our institution is driven by a resolute commitment to shaping the technology landscape of tomorrow and stands as a beacon of innovation in education.
